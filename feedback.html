STRESS

    The important thing that you should know about negative emotions, such as stress, is that they are feedback mechanisms created by evolution to preserve our species. They arise when something goes wrong, and thus warn is that something needs to be addressed (e.g., stress due to a deadline is to remind us not to forget it). The level of these emotions was set thousands of year ago in our species, when there was real danger. But now our species is in no danger of extinction, and so the level of emotions is higher than needed, i.e., most of our negative emotions are overreactions and hence detrimental.
